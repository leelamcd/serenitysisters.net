Basic Sitting Services

Overnight House-sitting: $25 / night
We’ll sleepover, bring in the mail, water the plants, and ensure safety and security to your home through the night.

Overnight Pet-Sitting: 1 Pet: $35 / night      2 or more Pets: $40 / night
 We’ll replenish your pets’ food and water, administer any medications, and deliver some serious TLC. (Lots of cuddling) How could we resist?

Daily Check-in: $20 / visit (pets or no pets)
We’ll come by, bring in the mail, water the plants, and do some light cleaning if needed. If you have pets, we’ll replenish their food and water, administer any medications, and pet/play with them for 30+ minutes.
